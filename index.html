The Firm
Cooperation and dedication are the drivers behind every successful assignment. We commit fully, or we do not commit at all.

Agathos Group provides discreet, high-end protective security services to a select number of government and institutional clients operating in complex environments worldwide.

The company was founded by principals whose backgrounds span both the corporate world and defence. Their collective experience encompasses senior roles in the private sector alongside maritime special operations, national-level counter-terrorism, and international task force deployments.

Our approach is shaped by direct experience in environments where the margin for error is zero.

The safety of human capital is not a secondary concern — it is the foundation upon which our clients' operations depend.

Mission

Our mission is to preserve and guarantee the safety of our clients. Operating in a global environment comes with increased risk across multiple domains. We exist to mitigate that risk decisively.

The demand for security grows in proportion to the complexity of the world we operate in. Those operating in high-stakes environments cannot afford a second-best response — and neither can we.

Approach

Discretion

Our clients operate in environments where visibility creates vulnerability. We design every engagement around the principle of minimal signature and maximum effect. Detailed credentials and personnel specifics are shared on a need-to-know basis during the vetting process.

Personnel

We select personnel for each assignment based on operational requirements, regional expertise, and language capability. Our officers hold credentials across tactical, protective, and corporate security disciplines.

Commitment

Results speak. Reputations are earned quietly. We deliver what we commit to, and we do not discuss our work publicly.
