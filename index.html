The Firm
Careful preparation and close cooperation underpin every assignment we take on.

Agathos Group provides discreet protective security services to a select number of government and institutional clients operating in complex environments worldwide.

The company was founded by principals whose backgrounds span both the corporate world and defence. Their collective experience includes roles in the private sector and direct operational backgrounds in special operations, national-level counter-terrorism, and international task force deployments.

The safety of human capital is not a secondary concern — it is the foundation upon which our clients' operations depend.

Mission

Our mission is to preserve the safety and security of our clients. Operating in a global environment comes with increased risk across multiple domains. We work to mitigate that risk.

Our approach is grounded in direct operational experience across a range of demanding environments.

The demand for security grows in proportion to global complexity. We work with a limited number of clients to ensure each engagement receives the attention it requires.

Training

We deliver specialist training programmes to military, law enforcement, and government security forces. Our instructors are practitioners — not career trainers — with direct operational backgrounds across a range of disciplines and environments.

We do not work from standing course material. Every programme is built from the ground up for the specific unit, mission set, and operating environment.

Assessment

We begin with a structured evaluation of the unit's current capability, organic resources, and the threat environment they operate in. This shapes all subsequent design decisions.

Curriculum Design

Instructors, content, duration, and delivery method are determined by the assessment findings — not by a pre-existing course catalogue.

Capability Transfer

Progress is evaluated throughout delivery. The goal is demonstrable capability that the unit owns and retains independently.

Approach
Results speak. Reputations are earned quietly.

Discretion

Our clients operate in environments where visibility creates vulnerability. We emphasize discretion, precision, and measurable results in every client interaction. Detailed credentials and personnel specifics are shared on a need-to-know basis during the vetting process.
